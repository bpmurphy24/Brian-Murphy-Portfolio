These generative design patterns were created in Photoshop using its ability to repeat a sequence of edits and effects. A particular effect that attracts my intrest is the reaction-diffusion textures which are replication of how chemicals react with eachother. These patterns can be found throughout biology in animals such as crustatians, insects, and even the villi in your body.
