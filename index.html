Multi-disciplinary artist and scientist specializing in GIS at Humboldt State. In addition to my science backgroud, I have experience in both physical and digital art. Most of my time now days is devoted to digital art such as graphic design, generative design, video and projection art and 3D modeling. My goal is to merge my artistic skills with my science background to create designs that can have a lasting positive impact on the interactions between people and the environment which they belong to.
In my free time I enjoy creating psychedelic music, creating video installations for music venues, as well as surf the internet to learn new skills and softwares to create art with.
